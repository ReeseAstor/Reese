About the Author

A Passion for Storytelling

Reese Astor's novels explore the alchemy between memory and imagination. Known for cinematic prose and intimate characters, Reese invites readers into richly textured worlds where every chapter feels inevitable yet surprising.

When not writing, Reese is usually sketching plotlines in sunlit cafes, cataloging vintage editions, or nurturing a sprawling greenhouse of herbs that inspire sensory detail on the page.
