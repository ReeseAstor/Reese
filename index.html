The Midnight Garden Reese Astor
"Some gardens only bloom at midnight, when the world has forgotten to watch."

About the Author

A Passion for Storytelling

Reese Astor is an award-winning author whose novels have touched readers across the globe. With a gift for creating deeply resonant characters and immersive worlds, Reese's writing explores themes of identity, belonging, and the transformative power of human connection.

When not writing, Reese can be found wandering through old bookshops, tending to an ever-growing collection of houseplants, or dreaming up the next story to share with the world.
